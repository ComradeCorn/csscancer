Cereal with cheese and sausages

A nice, nutritious, and easy midweek meal.

Total time: 12 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cereal
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 2 hot dogs
  • 1 slice of american cheese

Preparation

  1. Pour milk onto the cereal so that all the cereal is covered.

    Time: 12 minutes

  2. Place the two hot dogs on top and let sit for 5 hours.

    Time: 6 hours

  3. Garnish with the cheese.

    Time: 5 hours and 48 minutes
